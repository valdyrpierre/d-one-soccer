Sponsorship

The D-One Soccer League is seeking sponsorship from local businesses and organizations to support our league and its players. Sponsorship opportunities include team sponsorship, event sponsorship, and advertising on our website and promotional materials. If you are interested in becoming a sponsor, we would love to hear from you!

Sponsorship Options

We offer various sponsorship packages tailored to meet your business needs:

  • Website Sponsorship: Advertise your business on our website homepage for a fee of $10/day.
  • Social Media Sponsorship: Feature your company in our social media posts for $10/post.
  • Bundle Package: Combine website and social media sponsorship for $15/day (includes one social media post).

Sponsoring the D-One Soccer League is a great way to promote your business while supporting local soccer players and events. For more information or to discuss sponsorship opportunities, please fill ou our sponsorship form if you are interested and a member from our team will reach out.
