Meet Our Leadership Team

Driving the success of the D-One Soccer League

Alens Ace Cenatus

Founder and President

As the Founder and President of D-One Soccer League, Ace plays a pivotal role in the overall management and strategic direction of the organization. This dynamic leadership position encompasses a wide range of responsibilities across various departments, including Security, Marketing, Soccer Club/Team Management, and Vendor Management.

Paul Georges

Vice President and Commissioner

As the Vice President and Commissioner of D-One Soccer League, Paul oversees game day operations, player registration, player management (via the Tapin App), sales and promotion, finance, event planning, regulation compliance, and staff management. Paul is committed to fostering a culture of sportsmanship, teamwork, and excellence while promoting the growth of soccer in the community.

Nalby Junior Blot

Assistant Vice President

As the Assistant Vice President of D-One Soccer League, Nalby plays a pivotal role in the effective management and operation of the league. His responsibilities include league administration, vendor management, sponsorship, sales, and coordination with other league officials to ensure smooth functioning of daily operations and successful execution of league events.

Nixon Turenne

Head/Director of Security

As the Head of Security for D-One Soccer League, Nixon works closely with league members to develop safety plans and supervise security staff to ensure the safety and security of all league events, including matches, tournaments, and special events.

Geraldo Oriol

Security Staff

As a member of the security staff for D-One Soccer League, Geraldo follows security protocols and procedures to mitigate risks and maintain a safe environment for players, spectators, and staff.

Dominique Belizaire

Cash Processing Officer and Head of Concessions

Dominique plays a vital role in making supporters and spectators feel welcome. As the first face seen in the booth before entering the stadium, Dominique is responsible for sorting, counting, verifying, and packing cash collected from customers and vendors during matches, tournaments, and events.

Nirva VaVa Cooper

D-One Soccer League Host and Vendors

VaVa plays an exceptional role on match, tournament, or event days. She conducts post-game interviews with players and coaches, interacts with fans and league officials during matches and events, and reports feedback to the league. She works closely with the head of concessions for vendors and coordinates events for the league.
