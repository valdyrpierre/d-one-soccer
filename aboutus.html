D-One Soccer League

About the League

The purpose of D-One Soccer League is to promote fair play, sportsmanship, and community engagement through organized soccer competition for young adults aged 18 to 40.

The D-One Soccer League is an affiliate Member of Mass Soccer. Competitions sanctioned by the D-One Soccer League shall be considered sanctioned by Mass Soccer.
