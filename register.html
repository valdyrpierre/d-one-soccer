D-One Soccer League

Team Registration Information

  1. Teams must pay a $2,000 Registration Fee (Non-Refundable) to participate in the tournament.

    Teams can either pay the full amount upfront or opt for a payment plan with a $500 deposit.
    The remaining balance can be paid in three installments throughout the tournament.

    Accepted payment methods include:

    • Checks
    • Money Orders
    • Cash App
    • Zelle

    We also offer a payment plan.
    Click here to view the payment plan options.

  2. Cash Prizes:
    • 1st Place: $8,000
    • 2nd Place: $4,000

    Winners will also receive medals and trophies.

  3. Teams are required to pay a $125 referee fee before each game during the regular season.
    If your team qualifies for the playoffs, the league will cover your referee fees.

  4. D-One Soccer League is affiliated with Mass Soccer.
    Players already registered with Mass Soccer do not need to register again.

  5. D-One Soccer League is also affiliated with Tapin, an app that allows players and team owners to view game schedules, results, and statistics.

  6. D-One Soccer League is renowned for hosting some of the best finals in Massachusetts, with an average attendance of 3,000 fans.
    We strive to make the event bigger and better each year!

For more information or to register your team, please complete the registration form and email it to us.
